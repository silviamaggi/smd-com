I started my career as a designer and developer focussing on usability, accessibility and information architecture. For nine years, I lived in the UK, where I co-founded the web agency UI Farm and worked for clients spanning several industries, such as finance, retail and energy.

In 2020 I took a career break to deepen my knowledge and become a better and more conscious UX designer. Read more about my work history on LinkedIn.
