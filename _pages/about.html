For nine years, I lived and worked in the UK, where I co-founded the web agency UI Farm and worked in several fields, including the energy sector.
I started my career as a web designer and developer, wanting to build intuitive, usable and accessible interfaces. Gradually, I focused on user experience, bringing my attention from how things work to why.

My mission is to create interactions that serve people and businesses best, through careful research and design. At the beginning of 2020, I took a career break — disrupted by the pandemic and an international move — to deepen my knowledge and become a better, more conscious, UX designer. Read more about my work history on LinkedIn.

Interests

Photography is my life-long passion. Over the past few years, I contributed to several exhibitions, and authored two music videos for my husband’s debut album. In a bid to better my visual communication skills, I started learning to draw.

At the first hint of autumn, I often start knitting small accessories for my family and friends. Being close to nature is important to me. It’s where I can clear my mind, get inspired and relax.
